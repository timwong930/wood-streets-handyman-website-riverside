# AI Context — Wood Streets Handyman starter website

This repository is a public-facing local-business website. Keep the copy factual, local, and conversion-focused.

## Initial contact
- **Opener:** Hi, I’m Tim — I took a quick look at Wood Streets Handyman starter website and put together a simple idea for how the website could turn more visitors into calls or quote requests.
- **What to mention:**
  - Lead with making quote requests easier and the service list clearer.
  - Mention that handyman customers want a fast phone number, trust signals, and a simple path to request service.
- **Ask:** Offer a quick before/after screenshot and ask if they want a no-pressure walkthrough.
- **Tone:** Friendly, local, no-pressure, and short. Keep it practical and specific.
